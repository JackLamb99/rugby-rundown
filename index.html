Why we love Rugby Union

Teamwork

In rugby, teamwork is everything. Players work together seamlessly, relying on each other's strengths to drive the ball forward. From a coordinated attack to a solid defence, the game thrives on unity and collaboration, showcasing the power of collective effort.

Inclusiveness

Rugby is a sport where everyone is welcome and differences are celebrated, because it takes players all shapes, sizes, speeds and strength to drive a team to victory. Like unique gears in a well-oiled machine, all thatâ€™s required is a drive to keep pushing forward.

Respect

Respect is foundational in rugby. On and off the field, players uphold the value of sportsmanship, towards opponents and officials alike. Whether in victory or defeat, rugby fosters a culture of mutual respect, uniting competitors in a spirit of integrity.

History

Rugby boasts a rich and storied history spanning centuries. Originating in England in the 19th century, it has evolved into a global phenomenon, captivating players and fans worldwide with its tradition, innovation, and enduring spirit of competition.
